Quick answers

Do you take walk-ins?

Yes—when artists are available. For guaranteed time, use the booking page.

Do you require deposits?

Most custom tattoo appointments require a deposit to secure your slot. We’ll confirm details after you reach out.

What should I include in my message?

Style references, placement, approximate size, and any deadline. If it’s a cover-up, mention what’s underneath.

Piercing age requirements?

Please bring valid ID. If you’re under 18, requirements depend on the service—message us and we’ll confirm.
